Monthly Activities in CSTAD

Scholarship

On Saturday afternoon, December 09, 2023, the Center of Science and Technology Advanced Development (CSTAD) held an Entrance Exam for ITE 2nd Generation Scholarship Academic Year 2024 with many applicants from universities in Cambodia. The exam was supervised by the management team of CSTAD as the organizer of the exam committee and all staff as the exam committee members. The Exam took 3 hours and focused on the IT written test and English Essay written test. The exam ended with success, accuracy, and transparency. In order to promote ICT field in Cambodia and to align with the government's strategic plan as well as the Ministry Of Posts And Telecommunications, ISTAD provides the full scholarship for Information Technology training for 60 students for the academic year 2024.

Monthly Test

On Monday, October 30, 2023, CSTAD conducted the monthly examination for October 2023. The exam took 2 hours which consisted of Java and Web Design. The exam was conducted with accuracy and transparency. It should also be noted that in order to promote ICT Development aligned with the strategic plan of the Government, as well as the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, CSTAD has been teaching and providing 50 more IT scholarships for the academic year 2024. 11 months of specialized courses in Data Analytics, DevOps Engineering, Spring Advanced, Cyber ​​Security, Flutter Mobile Development. Besides the professional for IT scholarship training, CSTAD also provide another IT Fundamental Scholarship for 4 months training for 50 students on the basic training such as Java Programming ,Git ,Database essential ,Web Design, Software Development life Cycle, Final project

Sharing Experience Workshop on A Journey from Data Class to Data Career

November 17, 2023, Center of Science and Technology Advanced Development (CSTAD) hosted a workshop to share experiences about "A Journey from Data Class to Data Career" to all scholarship students of CSTAD with guidance and advice from Mr. Prem Leapheng who is a Data Scientist & Machine Learning. Throughout the program, there is a sharing of research methods, problem solving and preparation to the journey of this sector and skill. In addition, there are also a lot of questions from students with detailed explanations from the speaker as well. It should also be noted that the Center of Science and Technology Advanced Development not only provides IT professional scholarship training to Cambodian students but also connects all students to a good career and the opportunity to study abroad.

Snapshots Around CSTAD Activity

Sport day, Khmer New Year, Monthly Party,...

On Thursday and Friday, 10,11-August, 2023, The Center for Advanced Science and Technology Development (CSTAD) organized a two-day, one-night trip to Kep and Kampot after the successful completion of the 1st Generation of full Stack Web Development Training Course. The trip aims to increase brotherhood, entertainment and create memories, as well as relax, which students of the Center for Advanced Science and Technology Development (CSTAD) have made in the past several months in leaning, researching and bringing the good achievements on the projects include two projects related to education, one project related to design and photography, one project related to productivity, one project related to society and charity, one project related to business and one project related to business. For all the projects, the students could understand the whole project life cycle, from the requirements of the project, analysis and implementation to become usable projects. The trip was fun, memorable with a lot of good experiences. Center of Science and Technology Advanced Development (CSTAD) has successfully completed the first generation of Full Stack Web Development, with a total graduation of 42 students, 35 males and 7 females. They will proceed to advanced courses, studying one of the following subjects: - Flutter Mobile Development - Spring Advanced - Cybersecurity - DevOps Engineering - Blockchain Development - Data Analytics It should also be noted that in order to promote ICT Development aligned with the strategic plan of the Government, as well as the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, CSTAD has been teaching and providing 100 more IT scholarships for the academic year 2024. 11 months of specialized courses in Data Analytics, DevOps Engineering, Spring Advanced, Cyber ​​Security, Flutter Mobile Development. The Center will continue to provide high-quality training with new teaching methods, along with roadmaps in the field of information technology, and share with the government in training human resources in the field of information technology in Cambodia.
